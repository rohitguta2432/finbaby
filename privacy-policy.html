FinBaby Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 April 2026

FinBaby ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal finance tracker built by Rohit Raj. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data Storage

All your data — transactions, categories, budgets, and profile information — is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate any servers, cloud databases, or analytics services. No data ever leaves your phone.

SMS Access

FinBaby can read your SMS messages to auto-detect bank transactions. This feature is optional and requires your explicit permission. SMS messages are processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store on any server, or share your SMS data with any third party.

Biometric Data

FinBaby supports fingerprint and face authentication to lock the app. Biometric data is handled by your device's operating system. We do not access, store, or transmit any biometric information.

Financial Information

Transaction amounts, categories, notes, and budget data you enter are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Notifications

FinBaby may send local notifications for daily reminders and budget alerts. These are generated on your device and do not involve any external push notification services.

Data Export

You can export your transactions as a CSV file and create JSON backups. These files are saved to your device storage. You control where these files go.

Third-Party Services

FinBaby does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar service.

Children's Privacy

FinBaby is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
